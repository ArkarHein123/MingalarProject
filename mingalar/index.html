WE ARE MINGALAR MANDALAY

Mingalar Mandalay is situated next to the old Chanmyathazi Airport and across from the New Mandalay Stadium. With heritage sites Mahamuni Pagoda to the west and U Pain Bridge to the South within easy driving distance, Mingalar Mandalay is a development for the future built within the cultural heart of Mandalay and Myanmar. The development has been designed with a complete on-site utility offering, boasting its own water and electrical facilities, a 5-storey car park and even a firestation serving the wider community. With its roots in the past and its eyes on the future, this prime location is at the epicenter of both Mandalay’s and Myanmar’s incredible growth story.
